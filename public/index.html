Normality

Created with Tone.js, Tonal.js and Matter.js

In this audio-visual project, I wanted to dive into aspects of life which are accepted as normal. Including my own representations of mental health, the Human condition and a reflection on the health of our planet. This project is in the form of an EP where each piece is my own artistic commentary on these topics.
